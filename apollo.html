Objective

This proposal is presented to explore a hospital-supported financial literacy initiative at Apollo Hospital, with small-group, peer-led sessions for doctors.

What We Are Proposing

  • Small, closed-group RTMs for doctors.
  • Discussion-led sessions, not presentations.
  • Practical topics relevant across career stages.
  • No product promotion or selling.

Indicative Topics

  • Tax planning basics and choosing the appropriate tax regime.
  • Managing income, savings, and cash flows.
  • Insurance planning for doctors and families.
  • Long-term planning including children's education and retirement.
  • Common financial pitfalls doctors face and how to avoid them.

Why Partner with Finnovate?

  • Unbiased – advice is strictly outcome-focused with no product incentives.
  • Structured – a disciplined nine-step process based on the Six Pillars of Financial Fitness.
  • Holistic Perspective – we align goals, risks, investments, protection, taxes, and legacy planning for each doctor.
  • Personalized Advice – plans respect clinical schedules, family commitments, and unique priorities.
  • Quality & Research-Led – every recommendation is vetted by our Investment Advisory Committee.
  • Discipline & Accountability – implementation support and scheduled reviews keep goals on course.

Team Credentials

  • Founders: 45+ years of combined experience leading regulated advisory practices.
  • FinnFit Experts: CFP or NISM 10A/10B-certified professionals who craft doctor-specific financial roadmaps.
  • Portfolio Advisors: MBA/CFP/CFA qualified experts who ensure research-backed implementations.
  • Research Analysts: CFA, MBA, and NISM certified professionals providing research-backed recommendations across mutual funds and equities.
  • Hybrid Advisors: Experienced advisors blending online/offline engagement with cutting-edge technology.

Deliverables

  • Co-created RTM agenda tailored to Apollo doctors.
  • Small-group session facilitation with expert-led discussion.
  • Summary of key takeaways and practical action points.
  • Optional one-to-one follow-up slots for interested doctors.
