07Way Forward

PMS Licence

Finnovate has applied for its SEBI Portfolio Management Services licence — a significant step toward a regulated, fee-based, unbiased wealth management platform.

Licence Applied — Awaiting SEBI Approval
SEBI Portfolio Management Services Registration
Finnovate has applied for its SEBI PMS licence. Non-Discretionary PMS will launch upon approval, enabling Finnovate to operate as a fully regulated wealth management firm.
What is Non-Discretionary PMS?
  • Each client holds a dedicated regulated account in their own name
  • Finnovate advises on direct MFs, ETFs, bonds, and stocks — client retains final decision authority
  • Pre-agreed fee, zero-commission products only
Fee Model and Operating Flow
The fee calculation and fee deduction are done by the custodian. The custodian debits the client's account and credits Finovate's account as per the agreed fee structure. This creates a transparent operating model with no separate collection friction for the client or the team.
Why This Matters
  • Client assets held in their own NSDL/CDSL custodian account — full transparency, full ownership
  • Finnovate acts in a truly fiduciary capacity with fee-based, unbiased advice and no commission conflict
  • Enables advice across direct MFs, ETFs, bonds and stocks in one regulated account
  • Custodian-led fee deduction creates zero collection friction and zero operational bottleneck
  • This is an easier operational setup that helps Finovate scale faster while also establishing trust
  • Positions Finnovate as a fully regulated wealth manager with a significant competitive differentiator
08Corporate Governance

Governance & Compliance

Finnovate is built on a rigorous governance framework — regulated, independently audited, and professionally certified at every client-facing level.

  • 3-Level Accounting System — Finnovate maintains a structured three-tier accounting framework ensuring financial integrity, segregation of duties, and complete traceability across all transactions.
  • Audit by KDA — Accounts are independently audited by KDA, a recognised external audit firm, ensuring full compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements.
  • Half-Yearly & Annual Audits — In addition to the annual audit, a mid-year review is conducted every six months to maintain continuous oversight and early identification of any compliance gaps.
  • Ex-SEBI Officer for Compliance — Finnovate's compliance function is overseen by a former SEBI officer, bringing deep regulatory expertise and institutional-grade governance to the firm.
  • Certified Client-Facing Team — Every team member involved in client advisory holds active CFP (Certified Financial Planner), NISM Series 21A, and NISM Series 21B certifications — ensuring advice is delivered only by qualified professionals.
