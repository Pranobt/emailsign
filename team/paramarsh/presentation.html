06 — Way Forward
PMS Licence
Finnovate has applied for its SEBI Portfolio Management Services licence — a major competitive milestone.
📋
SEBI PMS Licence — Application Submitted
Non-Discretionary Portfolio Management Services · Status: In Progress
What is Non-Discretionary PMS?
  • Finnovate advises on direct MFs, ETFs, bonds & stocks — single regulated account
  • Client pays a pre-decided, mutually agreed fee
  • Finnovate recommends only zero-commission products
  • Client retains final decision authority — truly fiduciary
Why It Matters for Finnovate
  • Fee deducted by custodian (NSDL/CDSL) and credited to Finnovate — operationally seamless
  • Helps us scale faster — no manual billing or collection
  • Client assets in their own custodian account — full transparency
  • Significant competitive moat — fully regulated wealth manager
