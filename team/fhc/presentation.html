Customer Journey · Stage 1
Payment Done — the starting point
What the customer does
Visits ffc.html and pays ₹999
  • Sees the Financial Health Checkup offer
  • Fills name, email, phone number
  • Clicks "Start My Financial Checkup" → Cashfree payment page
  • Completes ₹999 payment → redirected to thank-you page with form link
What gets sent automatically
Invoice email + WhatsApp in seconds
  • WhatsApp: "Payment confirmed — here's your form link"
  • Email: GST invoice for ₹999 (via Mailercloud)
  • Zoho CRM: new Lead created, FHC Stage = Payment Done
UTM tracking
The source of every lead (Google Ad, Instagram, WhatsApp) is captured in UTM fields and saved on the record.
finnovate.in/ad/ffc.html
Customer Journey · Stage 2
Form Submitted — & RM assigned
The financial health form captures
6 sections of financial data
  • Personal & family details
  • Monthly income, expenses & savings rate
  • Existing investments & assets (MF, FD, gold, property)
  • Insurance: term cover, health cover, life policies
  • All loans & EMIs outstanding
  • Financial goals, timeline & concerns
RM Assignment (Auto · Round-Robin)
Aakanksha → Kruti → Mansi → repeat
  • System auto-assigns to the next RM in the cycle
  • RM gets email: "New lead assigned to you" with record link
  • Zoho Owner field updated to assigned RM
  • Customer auto-redirected to Calendly to book a call
After submission
The customer is automatically redirected to Calendly where they can pick a call slot. The form link stays active — customers can come back and re-fill if they missed something.
RM notification email includes
Everything you need to get started
  • Customer full name, email & phone
  • Payment order ID & date
  • Direct link → open their record in admin
Zoho CRM update
FHC Stage → Form Submitted
  • All form data synced to Lead/Contact record
  • Owner reassigned from David to the RM
  • Zoho workflow triggers customer email automatically
Customer Journey · Stage 3
Call Booked — prepare your brief
When customer books on Calendly
3 things happen automatically
  • FHC Stage in Zoho → Call Booked
  • Customer gets Calendly confirmation email + calendar invite
  • Admin dashboard record shows "Booked" with the call date/time
Your action — before the call
Open the Call Brief tab
  • Go to the record → Call Brief tab
  • Review FI Score, insurance gaps, goal timeline
  • Note the main areas of concern the customer mentioned
  • Prepare your talking points
If customer cancels
Stage → Meeting Cancelled. Zoho sends a "rebook anytime" email. The timeline shows a warning (!) on the Call step. Customer can rebook using their original link.
Call Brief tab shows you
Auto-generated financial summary
  • FI Score — how financially independent they are (%)
  • Emergency Fund — months covered (required vs current)
  • Term Cover Gap — how under-insured they are
  • Health Cover Gap — same for health insurance
  • Net Worth — total assets minus liabilities
  • Goals — stated financial goals & timeline
  • Areas of concern — what they're worried about
During Meeting tab
Use during & after the call
  • Record meeting outcome (interested, follow-up needed, etc.)
  • Notes from the conversation
  • Any corrections to financial data from the customer

Admin Console
The Dashboard — your home screen
What you see
All your assigned leads (analysts) or all leads (admin)
  • Customer name, email, phone
  • Payment date & order ID
  • Status badges: Pay / Form / Call / Report / Zoho
  • Click Open → to go to the full record
Filters — find what you need fast
8 filter options including Stage
  • Stage — filter by journey stage (e.g. "Call Booked")
  • Form — Submitted or Pending
  • Report — Not Started / In Progress / Ready / Shared
  • Call — Not Booked / Booked / Completed / No Show
  • Date range, search by name/email/phone
Quick tabs at the top
Use Pending forms, Report queue, and Booked calls tabs as shortcuts to common filtered views.
